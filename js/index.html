The Band

Formed in 2010, Playing for Kicks have taken our eclectic assortment of rock and pop covers as far afield as France and Wales, but mainly all corners of Bradford. Over the years we’ve said goodbye to a Dave and Nige and hello to Jonathan but we still get a huge kick out of playing!

Mike

Guitar/Vocals

plays the easy bits and leaves the tricky stuff to others, mainly so he can bop about, while imagining he’s in front of the mirror with a hairbrush.

Dave

Bass/Backing

would like to be a lead guitarist, but keeps a lid on his ambition to provide a rock solid bass and a reliable enthusiasm.

Cath

Drums/Vocals

feeds us with her energy and is never happier than when she’s bashing away at the back... as long as she gets to keep her Madonna mic!

Jonathan

Keys/Backing

plays pretty much anything, but for now he's been chained to the keyboards. It's rumoured that his microphone is just there for decoration.
