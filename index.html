The Band

Formed in 2010, Playing for Kicks have taken our eclectic assortment of rock and pop covers as far afield as France and Wales, but mainly all corners of Bradford. Our line-up has changed a little over the years, though our singer and drummer have been in it for the long haul. We're also really pleased that following a (brief) residency in Scotland, Dave has rejoined us.

Mike

Guitar/Vocals

plays the easy bits and leaves the tricky stuff to others, mainly so he can bop about, while imagining he’s in front of the mirror with a hairbrush.

Ed

Bass

is rumoured to be one of the tallest bassists in the country. He brings a wealth of experience and a knowledge of 1990s music that has previously been unknown to the rest of the band.

Cath

Drums/Vocals

feeds us with her energy and is never happier than when she’s bashing away at the back... ahem...

Jonathan

Keys/Backing

plays pretty much anything, but for now he's been chained to the keyboards. It's rumoured that he does actually use the microphone and it isn't just there for decoration...

Dave

Guitar

does all that clever stuff on guitar that you didn't realise was there until it wasn't. Welcome back!
